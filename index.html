Latest Updates

  • Oct 3, 2025: The national government released ₱1.625 billion to boost relief and recovery operations in Cebu. (The Manila Times)
  • Oct 3, 2025: The President pledged ₱150 million in aid for quake-affected areas. (Philstar)
  • Oct 3, 2025: The PCSO mobilized relief goods and evacuation kits via Coast Guard vessels and flights. (Philippine News Agency)
  • Oct 3, 2025: Singapore Red Cross committed SGD 50,000 (≈ ₱2.2 million) to support medical, hygiene, and psychosocial relief efforts. (GMA Network)
  • Oct 3, 2025: Local governments and agencies deployed over 113,000 relief items (water, rice packs, food packs, hygiene kits) to northern Cebu towns including Bogo. (SunStar Publishing Inc.)
  • Oct 3, 2025: The government declared state of calamity for affected municipalities (Bogo, San Remigio, Tabuelan, Medellin) to facilitate faster release of funds and aid. (Philippine Information Agency)
  • Oct 3, 2025: Repair and restoration efforts: power is being restored in Bogo City. (Philippine Commission on Orders)
  • Oct 3, 2025: Authorities clarified that no permit is required for individuals/groups transporting relief goods to northern Cebu—aid delivery is encouraged. (GMA Network)
